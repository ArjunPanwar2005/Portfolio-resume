Successful learning requires both diligent study and practical on-the-job experience. Our decisions and experiences shape our future. Co-Founder

ZelioTech Inc

Co-Founder

A tech Start-up to make Apps for Android and iOS like LocShare, Bloozap which are successfully published on Playstore.
LocShare: LocShare lets you share your location with your friends so that they reach your exact location. The app uses the GPS co-ordinates to get your location and the Haversine formula for calculating the distance between your current location and the target Location. Bloozap: Bloozap is a short Distance Chatting app which uses Bluetooth to communicate.

Robotic Hand Using Flex Sensors

Co-Founder

Made a Robotic hand that can be controlled by a sensor glove (Based on the gesture movement of glove while you are wearing it) with flex sensors attached to the back of fingers. Flex means bend, this is the sensors that change the resistance depending on the amount of bend on the sensor. Data from the sensors sent to the Control unit are digitally converted and then displayed as a text on the L.E.D.

Bristle Bot

Founder

This bot consists of 2 toothbrushes, They are attached in such a way so that the bot can be directed in one particular direction. A battery is attached so as to provide an external power for its running . The pager motor's vibrations flow down the bristles, causing the brush to slide and spin on the surface.It uses IR sensors so it can be controlled using light in the dark (Light following)
