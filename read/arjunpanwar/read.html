Community Service

  • Home
  • » Community Service

“Remember that the happiest people are not those getting more, but those giving more.”

— H. Jackson Brown Jr

Community service gives students a chance to get involved in their region for a social cause and has a long-lasting, beneficial effect on society as a whole. Students acquire life skills and mental satisfaction via community work or volunteering while helping people in need. There are dozens of organisations throughout the globe that interact with and hold millions of young people. People of various ages participate in community service, with youth making up the majority of participants. Social service agencies, non-profit bodies, and groups accept volunteers of all ages fall under the category of community-based organisations. People with kind hearts understand the essence of generosity and benevolence and believe giving back to the society through social service. I regard community service as an essential commitment to be performed as a regular routine responsibility. I have firm belief that it must be a duty for every human being to be genuinely involved according to his capabilities. I love to contribute in volunteering and performing tasks and each time I had feeling of selflessness and being compassionate. It gives me immense pleasure and delight in giving back to the community for those who really are deprived sections of our society. It inculcates a sense of social accountability and enhanced personal growth through moral values and made me more responsible, more sincere and more organized. The significance of volunteering emerges from the sacrifice of time it made to support a cause or the community.
