“I believe in innovation and that the way you get innovation is you fund research and you learn the basic facts.”

— Bill Gates

Research is creating new knowledge. Curiosity fuels it: we get intrigued, pose questions, and immerse ourselves in understanding and absorbing everything there is to explore. Research broadens our understanding and provides us with the most up-to-date knowledge. It teaches us to be more selective as researchers are able to investigate methodologies with greater accuracy. Persistent learning is thriving and flourishing. I had been fortunate to have guidance, counseling, regular directions and gentle support from my school teachers, mentors and senior professors under whose experienced supervision I was able to publish some of my very notable works in form of publications on an open source. I would like to invite all visitors to my website to take a few minutes to have a look at my innovative attempts in order to motivate me to investigate more in latest research ventures in subsequent years.
